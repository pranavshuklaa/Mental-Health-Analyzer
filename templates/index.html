Given below are steps on how to Perfectly analyze your mental health status! Dont worry , there are just 3 steps !



1

Step-1

As a newcomer to our page, you may have some questions and doubts about mental health and mental illness. To answer that curiosity, check out our chatbot. It's a ML-based conversational chatbot that you can talk to as if you were talking to a friend who has knowledge about mental health and mental illness. Just make sure your questions aren't too complex, as the chatbot may get confused. After having a fun and informative conversation with our chatbot, feel free to explore our website or take the big step and analyze your mental health by taking one of our tests

2

Step-2

Take a test by clicking on the "Questionnaires" button in the navigation bar or by clicking below. You'll be taken to a page where you can choose from 5 different mental illnesses to test for. After completing the test, you'll see your severity level for that specific mental illness, which ranges from No Illness to Severe. Once you have your results, you can find solutions in our "Solutions" page.

3

Step-3

Once you've taken the test and received your severity level, don't worry, we've got you covered! Whether your results show No Illness or Severe Illness, we have information and solutions for you. Find out more about the illness and how to deal with it by reading the solutions we have provided for every severity level. Implement the solutions, take more tests, and find out your complete mental health status!
